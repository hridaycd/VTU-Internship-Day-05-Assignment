College Event Management Dashboard

This dashboard provides a centralized view of all college events, their schedules, and participant information. Effective event management is essential for colleges to foster campus culture, enhance student engagement, and coordinate academic and extracurricular activities. It helps administration track registrations, allocate resources, and ensure smooth execution of seminars, cultural fests, and technical competitions.
